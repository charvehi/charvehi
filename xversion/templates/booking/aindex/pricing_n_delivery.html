Pricing and Delivery

{% endblock %}

The pricing of the models is regulated by yaanrides.com. We offer lowest price of the particular model. The prices which are displayed on the website are less than offline price. In addition, we give discount and coupon on rides.

We deliver motorbikes through our partner Fleet-Masters. The riders are provided location of the hosted model with all the necessary details. The Fleet Master is also notified of the booking for ease and transparency. We are operating with on-demand delievery services for some models.
